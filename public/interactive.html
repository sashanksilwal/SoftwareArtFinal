However, poetry is an escape tunnel that goes beyond the natural usage of a language.
"Even within the strict rules and regulations of software, style, manner, and method
are distinctive, varied, and personal." The uniqueness and character that the author gives
to their code, making the code invoke a certain kind of reaction or emotion from the
reader makes it a poem. Moreover, the leverage utilized by coders to go beyond the
language and use creativity in variable names, choices of structure, or hidden
meaning/messages makes it a poem.
